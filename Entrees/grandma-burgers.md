# Grandma Burgers
## Delicious BBQ Burgers, no bun needed

### Ingredients
#### Burgers
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 onion, diced
- 1/2 tsp salt
- pepper, a few shakes

#### Sauce
- 1/4 cup worchestershire sauce
- 1/2 Tbsp vinegar
- 1/4 cip sugar
- 1 cup ketchup

1. Brown and pour off grease
2. Pour on sauce and let simmer for 10 mins
